Badge Information

Criteria for "Brillian ICM Modul Germany" - What it takes to earn this badge

Successful completion of the serious game brillianICM which includes the following activities:

  • Theory module Intercultural Management

  • Fundamental classification of Intercultural Management

  • Classification of Germany in a cultural map

  • Guessing the 9 dimension values of Germany

  • Explanation and execution of the Four Sides Model of Friedemann Schulz von Thun

  • Classification of the visible and invisible parts of the Iceberg Model

  • Multiple choice quizzes regarding the behavior in the following situations:

    • Greeting, First contacts with new colleagues, Appropriate topics to talk about, Soccer clubs,
      Punctuality, Reactions to sick notes, Hiring Criterias, Extra hours, Substitution, Choosing a way of transportation,
      Car renting, the Autobahn, Bonus sharing, Smalltalk, Table manners
